About ManusAI Design

ManusAI Design merges the Latin word "manus" (hand) with artificial intelligence to create a tool that feels like an extension of your creative vision. We specialize in minimalist black and white line-style logos that are timeless, versatile, and instantly recognizable.

Our AI has been trained on thousands of minimalist designs to understand the principles of simplicity, balance, and meaningful negative space.
